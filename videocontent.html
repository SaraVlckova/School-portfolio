Introduction

In this project we were working on video content for CPH Commune to improve their international biking guide and create informative website with video content. Project was supposed to learn us how to choose what to record to say what we want. We have learned to use basic and advanced camera equipment and then following final edit in programs.

Task

Our task was record video, which we will gonna use in project under basic content theme. Recorded video were supposed to fit in content on website.

Challenges

Work with advanced equipment I had choose - drone and osmo. Other challenge was edit it in program we never used before.

Gained Skills

Group working, Video production, Driving drone, Video editing

PROCESS

We start with creation of team canvas with our personal and team goals, our rules and expectations.

Next step was decision about concept. Our team definitely agreed on teaser about biking in CPH for tourists, international students or exchange student.

Under teaser we meant show tour of friends through CPH and show nice places in connection to show behaviour on roads, which we explain on website lately.

After we planed all scenes, we choose right equipment and start recording. We decided for Mobile Osmo, Osmo+ and Drone. With both Osmos we were able to make nice shots from city, while driving and environment around to make watcher more emphatic with experience. And Drone we decided to use to break usual videos and bring a bit of air to video.
